WHY FASHION?

The brilliant colors, the exciting textures, and the dynamic energy as clothes transform their wearers. Even as a child, I was always fascinated by fashion. Capturing it all led me to photography, so the two coming together is truly magical.

I have been doing fashion photography professionally for 7 years now, although I have been pursuing it since my teens, and through my years at the Venston Bay School for the Arts where I majored in Fashion Photography.

Fashion is truly my passion. Now let me show you what I mean.
